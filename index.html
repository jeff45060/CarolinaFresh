About Carolina Fresh Market & More



Hi, my name is Tiffany Albertson and I would like to tell you a little about myself. I am married to Al Albertson and we have two wonderful children. Al was born and raised in the Pelzer/Williamston SC area and is the owner of Alberson's Garage & towing and K&B towing. I was raised by my grandparents and my daddy in Anderson SC. My grandparents were farmers and owned a welding shop. They showed me the value of agriculture and hard work. In high-school, I became the first female FFA president of the Anderson Extension Campus chapter. I received the highest award from the National FFA organization.

Carolina Fresh Market & More opened on April 8th, 2017. We opened the store to serve our community by offering the best homegrown products we can find. We buy from local farmers and grow our own produce as well. We offer organic meats and dairy products. Our children love to help with the animals and gardening. We are raising them to know you can do anything, if you put your mind to it.

The main-focus of the store is to offer healthier eating options and provide products that are all natural. We also carry products from local craft makers. We have beef hotdogs and snow cones. We are planning to open a farm to table restaurant in the future.

I hope you take time to come by and see us. We are open year around.

Tiffany Alberson
